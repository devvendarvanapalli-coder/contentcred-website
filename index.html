Your campaign requires a minimum $5,000 clipping budget. This is not a fee. It is a performance reserve held to pay clippers for the views they deliver. You control it at all times.

Our fee is 25–35% of your clipping budget, confirmed before launch. It covers campaign architecture, clipper recruitment, clip production, and performance tracking. Agreed upfront, no surprises.
